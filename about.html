Good design is like a good friend. It excites, inspires, and delights. It's reliable, silent at all the right times, but also there to offer a helping hand if you need one. I'm Bianca, and I create good design.

Most people call me B, just the singular letter because it's short and fun, kind of like me. I grew up singing classical voice and opera, and I studied Chemistry at UT Austin before becoming a designer. Graphic design was my starting point, and I eventually ended up in UX Design. It bridged the creative and analytical parts of me, closing that gap with a career I find engaging and fulfilling.

When I'm not messing around in design software or playing with sticky notes, I love spending time with my two dogs, practicing coffee brewing techniques, and hoarding super cute stationery. Having fun in my life and career is important to me, and I try to enjoy both to the fullest extent.
